
1. Energy & Water Conservation
Use LED bulbs & energy-efficient appliances.

Turn off lights/electronics when not in use.

Fix leaking taps to save water.

2. Mindful Shopping
Buy only what you need (avoid impulse purchases).

Choose products with minimal packaging.

Opt for durable goods over disposable ones.

3. Digital Reduction
Reduce paper use by going digital (e-bills, e-tickets).

Delete unnecessary emails/files to lower digital carbon footprint.

4. Food Waste Reduction
Plan meals to avoid overbuying groceries.

Store food properly to extend shelf life.

Compost organic waste instead of throwing it away.
